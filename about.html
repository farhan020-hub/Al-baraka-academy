About our School

Welcome to Al-Baraka Light Academy, where we combine quality, affordable education with strong Islamic values. We believe in nurturing young minds to realize their full potential, offering a blend of contemporary education alongside an Islamic curriculum. At Al-Baraka Light Academy, we are to shaping future leaders with integrity and a deep sense of character, knowledge, and success. Located in Olekasasi B, off Rimpa Road, we provide a safe and supportive environment where your child can grow intellectually and spiritually. Our programs span from Day Care, Pre-School, Lower and Upper Primary, to specialized Madrassa classes, Quranic education, and a Tahfiid program. Whether your child is beginning their educational journey or continuing to build upon foundational skills, Al-Baraka Light Academy offers a comprehensive learning experience that integrates modern educational standards with Islamic teachings.
